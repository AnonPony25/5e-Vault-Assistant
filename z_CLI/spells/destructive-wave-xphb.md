# Destructive Wave
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*5th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Destructive energy ripples outward from you in a 30-foot [Emanation](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each creature you choose in the [Emanation](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a target takes `5d6` Thunder damage and `5d6` Radiant or Necrotic damage (your choice) and has the [Prone](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition. On a successful save, a target takes half as much damage only.

**Classes**: [Cleric (Tempest Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-tempest-domain.md "class=XPHB"); [Paladin](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 261*
