# Spare the Dying
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*cantrip, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 15 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature within range that has 0 [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) and isn't dead. The creature becomes [Stable](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/stable-xphb.md).

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).

**Classes**: [Artificer](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-artificer.md); [Bard (College of Lore)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-college-of-lore-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Cleric (Death Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-death-domain-dmg.md "subclass=DMG;class=XPHB"); [Cleric (Grave Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-grave-domain-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB"); [Cleric (Nature Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-nature-domain.md "class=XPHB"); [Cleric](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB")
