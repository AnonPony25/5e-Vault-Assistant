It rolls initiative to determine when it acts. It disappears after 1 minute, when it is reduced to 0 hit points, or when Kalain dies or falls [unconscious](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious).

***Taunt (2/Day).*** Kalain can use a bonus action on her turn to target one creature within 30 feet of it.
