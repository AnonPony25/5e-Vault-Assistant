## Traits

***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny raven. The swarm can't regain [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or gain [Temporary Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).

***Beaks.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* `+4`, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 5 (`1d6 + 2`) Piercing damage, or 2 (`1d4`) Piercing damage if the swarm is [Bloodied](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied).
