# Swarm of Lemures
*Source: Monster Manual (2024) p. 194*  

When devils drive hosts of lemures into close proximity or when lemures compress into a single mass, a swarm forms and adopts a unified mind.

## Lemures

*Devils of Agony and Despair*

- **Habitat.** Planar (Nine Hells)  
- **Treasure.** None  

The least of all devils, lemures arise from wicked souls, their mortal memories scoured away. Only vague limbs and anguished features jut from these slurries of infernal proto-matter.

## Traits

***Hellish Restoration.*** If the swarm dies in the Nine Hells, it revives with all its [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) in `1d10` days unless it is killed by a creature under the effects of a [Bless](z_CLI/spells/bless-xphb.md) spell or its remains are sprinkled with [Holy Water](z_CLI/items/holy-water-xphb.md).

***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through an opening large enough for a Medium creature. The swarm can't regain [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or gain [Temporary Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).
