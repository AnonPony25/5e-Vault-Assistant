# Port Nyanzaru Encounters
*Source: Tomb of Annihilation p. 193* 

**Port Nyanzaru Encounters**

| dice: d20 | Encounter |
|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | A parrot poops on a random character's head. |
| 2 | Near the harbor, an [ankylosaurus](z_CLI/bestiary/beast/ankylosaurus-xmm.md) that tows boats through the warehouse canals goes on a rampage and must be calmed, restrained, or killed. |
| 3 | A merchant shouts, "Stop! Thief!" as a furtive youngster ([commoner](z_CLI/bestiary/humanoid/commoner-xmm.md)) rushes past the characters. If the thief is caught, the grateful merchant can introduce characters to a merchant prince or provide them one other favor. |
| 4 | A drunk foreign sailor (unarmored [veteran](z_CLI/bestiary/humanoid/warrior-veteran-xmm.md)) is loudly trying to pick a fight with three local [commoners](z_CLI/bestiary/humanoid/commoner-xmm.md), who are clearly no match for the lout. If the characters intervene, the grateful locals become a reliable source of information about Port Nyanzaru, but the sailor and his shipmates ambush the characters elsewhere in the city later on. |
| 5 | A beggar ([commoner](z_CLI/bestiary/humanoid/commoner-xmm.md)) grabs a character by the arm and shouts, "The ancient one beneath the Forbidden City gives birth to a terrible new god! The snake-men know! They know!" Then he stumbles away into the crowd. Passersby tell the adventurers to ignore the beggar, and that his predictions are wrong most of the time. |
| 6 | Alarm horns declare that undead are attacking Malar's Throat. Mercenaries keep the monsters out of the city proper, but people are trapped in the temple of Tymora and besieged by `2d6` [zombies](z_CLI/bestiary/undead/zombie-xmm.md) and `2d6` [skeletons](z_CLI/bestiary/undead/skeleton-xmm.md) led by a [ghoul](z_CLI/bestiary/undead/ghoul-xmm.md). If the characters defeat the undead, the temple rewards them with 5 gallons of tej and a [potion of healing](z_CLI/items/potion-of-healing-xdmg.md). |
| 7 | Shouts of "Look out!" give a character a brief warning as a water barrel, building stone, or other heavy weight crashes down. The character must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or be struck, taking 14 (`4d6`) bludgeoning damage. Any character who succeeds on a DC 15 Wisdom ([Perception](z_CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception)) check spots a disguised [yuan-ti pureblood](z_CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/yuan-ti-infiltrator-xmm.md) slinking away from the scene. |
| 8 | A [tabaxi minstrel](z_CLI/bestiary/humanoid/tabaxi-minstrel-toa.md) asks one of the characters for a gold piece to stake her for gambling, promising to pay it back. If the character agrees, the tabaxi makes good on her promise the next day, returning a pouch containing 10 gp. |
| 9 | A thief ([spy](z_CLI/bestiary/humanoid/spy-xmm.md)) tries to pick the pocket of one of the characters. |
| 10 | The characters run into [Volothamp Geddarm](z_CLI/bestiary/npc/volothamp-volo-geddarm-toa.md), who is delivering a copy of his new book to one of the city's merchant princes. There is a 50 percent chance that Volo is drunk. |
| 11-20 | The characters meet an NPC with a side quest |
