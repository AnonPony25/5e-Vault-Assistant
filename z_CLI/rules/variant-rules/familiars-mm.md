# Familiars
*Source: Monster Manual p. 347* 

Any spellcaster that can cast the [find familiar](z_CLI/spells/find-familiar-xphb.md) spell (such as an apprentice, warlock, or wizard) is likely to have a familiar. The familiar can be one of the creatures described in the spell (see the "Player's Handbook") or some other Tiny monster, such as a [crawling claw](z_CLI/bestiary/undead/crawling-claw-xmm.md), an [imp](z_CLI/bestiary/fiend/imp-xmm.md), a [pseudodragon](z_CLI/bestiary/dragon/pseudodragon-xmm.md), or a [quasit](z_CLI/bestiary/fiend/quasit-xmm.md).

> [!note]
> This variant is intended for NPCs. (https://twitter.com/JeremyECrawford/status/920714594334654465)
