***Cunning Action.*** On each of its turns, Orvex can use a bonus action to take the [Dash](z_CLI/rules/actions.md#Dash), [Disengage](z_CLI/rules/actions.md#Disengage), or [Hide](z_CLI/rules/actions.md#Hide) action.

***Sneak Attack (1/Turn).*** Orvex deals an extra 7 (`2d6`) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Orvex that isn't [incapacitated](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) and Orvex doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.
