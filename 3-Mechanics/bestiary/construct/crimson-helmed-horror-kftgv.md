## Traits

***Magic Resistance.*** The helmed horror has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

***Spell Immunity.*** The helmed horror is immune to [burning hands](z_CLI/spells/burning-hands-xphb.md), [fireball](z_CLI/spells/fireball-xphb.md), and [scorching ray](z_CLI/spells/scorching-ray-xphb.md).

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The helmed horror makes two longsword attacks.

***Flaming Longsword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `+6` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (`1d8 + 4`) fire damage, or 9 (`1d10 + 4`) fire damage if used with two hands.

## Bonus Actions

***Conjure Sword.*** As a bonus action on its first turn in combat, the helmed horror conjures a longsword-sized blade of fire, which appears in its free hand. This sword disappears in a cloud of smoke when the helmed horror drops to 0 hit points.
