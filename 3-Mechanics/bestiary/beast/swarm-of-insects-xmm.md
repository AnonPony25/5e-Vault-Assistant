## Traits

***Spider Climb.*** If the swarm has a [Climb Speed](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/climb-speed-xphb.md), the swarm can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny insect. The swarm can't regain [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or gain [Temporary Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).
