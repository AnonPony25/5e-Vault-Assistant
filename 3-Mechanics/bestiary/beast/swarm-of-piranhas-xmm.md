## Traits

***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny piranha. The swarm can't regain [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or gain [Temporary Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).

***Water Breathing.*** The swarm can breathe only underwater.
