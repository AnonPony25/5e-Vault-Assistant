# Barkskin
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of oak bark)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin assumes a bark-like appearance, and the target has an [Armor Class](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) of 17 if its AC is lower than that.

**Classes**: [Bard (College of Lore)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-college-of-lore-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Bard](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Nature Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-nature-domain.md "class=XPHB"); [Druid](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Ranger](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md)
