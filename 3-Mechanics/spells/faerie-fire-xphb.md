# Faerie Fire
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Objects in a 20-foot [Cube](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md) within range are outlined in blue, green, or violet light (your choice). Each creature in the [Cube](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md) is also outlined if it fails a Dexterity saving throw. For the duration, objects and affected creatures shed [Dim Light](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) in a 10-foot radius and can't benefit from the [Invisible](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) condition.

[Attack](z_CLI/rules/actions.md#Attack) rolls against an affected creature or object have [Advantage](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) if the attacker can see it.

**Classes**: [Artificer](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-artificer.md); [Bard (College of Lore)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-college-of-lore-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Bard](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Light Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-light-domain-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Cleric (Twilight Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-twilight-domain-tce.md "subclass=TCE;class=XPHB"); [Druid](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Ranger (Swarmkeeper)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-swarmkeeper-tce.md "subclass=TCE;class=XPHB"); [Warlock (Archfey Patron)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-archfey-patron-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB")
