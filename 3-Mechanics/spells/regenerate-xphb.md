# Regenerate
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*7th-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a prayer wheel)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

A creature you touch regains `4d8 + 15` [Hit Points](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). For the duration, the target regains 1 [Hit Point](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) at the start of each of its turns, and any severed body parts regrow after 2 minutes.

**Classes**: [Bard](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB")

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 311. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
