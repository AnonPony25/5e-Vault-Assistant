# Greater Restoration
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*5th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and magically remove one of the following effects from it:

- 1 [Exhaustion](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level  
- The [Charmed](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) or [Petrified](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Petrified) condition  
- A curse, including the target's [Attunement](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/attunement-xphb.md) to a cursed magic item  
- Any reduction to one of the target's ability scores  
- Any reduction to the target's [Hit Point](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum
