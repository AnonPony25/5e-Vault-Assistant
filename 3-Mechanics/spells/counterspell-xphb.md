# Counterspell
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 60 feet of yourself casting a spell with Verbal, Somatic, or Material component
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell. The creature makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the spell dissipates with no effect, and the action, [Bonus Action](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), or [Reaction](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) used to cast it is wasted. If that spell was cast with a spell slot, the slot isn't expended.
