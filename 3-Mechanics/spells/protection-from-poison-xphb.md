# Protection from Poison
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a creature and end the [Poisoned](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition on it. For the duration, the target has [Advantage](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end the [Poisoned](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition, and it has [Resistance](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Poison damage.

**Classes**: [Artificer](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-artificer.md); [Bard (College of Lore)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-college-of-lore-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Bard](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Paladin](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md); [Ranger](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB")
