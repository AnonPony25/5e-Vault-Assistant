# Flame Strike
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*5th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of sulfur)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A vertical column of brilliant fire roars down from above. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high [Cylinder](z_CLI/rules/variant-rules/cylinder-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on a point within range makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking `5d6` Fire damage and `5d6` Radiant damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The Fire damage and the Radiant damage increase by `1d6` for each spell slot level above 5.

**Classes**: [Bard](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Light Domain)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-light-domain-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Cleric](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid (Circle of Wildfire)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-circle-of-wildfire-tce.md "subclass=TCE;class=XPHB"); [Paladin (Oath of Devotion)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-oath-of-devotion-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-divine-soul-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB"); [Warlock (The Genie)](z_CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-the-genie-tce.md "subclass=TCE;class=XPHB")
