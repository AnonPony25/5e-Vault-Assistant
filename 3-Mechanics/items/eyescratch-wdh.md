# Eyescratch
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](z_CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Poison)

A creature that comes into contact with this poison must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be [poisoned](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 1 hour and [blinded](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded) while [poisoned](z_CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way. A [lesser restoration](z_CLI/spells/lesser-restoration-xphb.md) spell or similar magic ends the effect.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dragon Heist p. 211*
